About Samanway

The Students’ Council, Indian Institute of Science is organizing Samanway – an Industry-Academia interaction event. The event will host talks by eminent people in the industry and will be one of the first initiatives to provide a platform for students to interact with industry experts about research opportunities in niche sectors of science. The companies that conduct research in science generally face a dearth of skilled candidates with scientific temper in their human resource from India, which the event seeks to address by opening up avenues for future employee-employer relations.

To cover different areas of scientific research effectively, Samanway will be organized in three parts:

Part I: Pure Sciences This event will be focused on industrial research in pure sciences (Biology, Physics, Chemistry)

Part II: Computational Engineering This part will focus on applied mathematics research including but not limited to computer science, simulations, modeling, communication and information technology, design and imaging.

Part III: Production and Manufacturing This part will focus on science and engineering disciplines including but not limited to production technology, manufacturing, robotics, and AI.

While Samanway is organized in parts, each part is designed to attract students from all branches of scientific research. IISc is home to a students enrolled in Master's degree programs in Engineering, an integrated Ph.D. (post-B.Sc.) program in Sciences and Ph.D. programs in a wide spectrum of disciplines in science and engineering. The first batch of students from the 4-year B. S. program is graduating this year with a fundamental training in science and exposure to research.

Samanway 2014 thus would provide an opportunity to the participating companies to reach out to a multifarious talent pool
